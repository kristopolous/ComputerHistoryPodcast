"Great ideas usually appear years - sometimes decades - before they can become commercial products. It takes that long to bring the cost of a high-tech product down to where the masses can afford it, and it can take even longer before those masses finally perceive a personal or business need for the product"

Mark Stephens (under the penname Robert X Cringley) in The Afterword of the Harpers-Business First Edition of Accidental Empires, 1993.

He ends the next paragraph with the perennial advice: "The technology is already there, we just have to find it". So today we continue the quest I started on June 9th in episode 1.1, today we finally resume looking for our telegraph.
